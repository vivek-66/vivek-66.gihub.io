Trending manga's in 2023

1. "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba" - A fantasy action series about a young boy named Tanjiro Kamado who becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and find a cure for his sister, who has been turned into a demon.
2. "My Hero Academia" - This manga follows the story of Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers in a world where nearly everyone has them. He dreams of becoming a hero and enrolls in a prestigious hero academy.
3. "One Piece" - A long-running adventure series about Monkey D. Luffy and his crew as they search for the ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in a world of pirates and powerful adversaries.
4. "Attack on Titan" (Shingeki no Kyojin) - A dark fantasy manga set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight the Titans.
5. "Jujutsu Kaisen" - This manga revolves around Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of curses and sorcery after swallowing a cursed object containing a powerful curse.
6. "Haikyuu!!" - A sports manga centered around high school volleyball and the journey of Shoyo Hinata, a short but talented player who aims to become the best.
7. "Black Clover" - The story of Asta, a boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything. He aims to become the Wizard King despite his lack of magical abilities.
8. "Solo Leveling" - A webtoon that follows Sung Jin-Woo, who becomes a "player" in a world where portals to dungeons filled with monsters have opened, granting people superhuman powers.
9. "Chainsaw Man" - A dark fantasy manga about a young devil hunter named Denji, who makes a contract with a devil and becomes a devil hunter to pay off his debts.
10. "Dr. Stone" - A sci-fi adventure manga where humanity has been turned to stone, and Senku Ishigami, a genius scientist, sets out to rebuild civilization using scientific knowledge.
Please note that the popularity of manga can change rapidly, so it's best to check the latest sources like manga sales rankings, online platforms, and community forums to find the most trending ones as of the current date.
